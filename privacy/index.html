    Privacy and Consent¶

    2024.1

    rovera is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers.

    Policy Statements¶

    rovera policy requires that:

    (a) Privacy policy shall be made available to inform Customers how rovera collects, uses, secures and shares customer information.

    (b) Valid consent must be obtained for data collected from a Customer and the purposes data is used for must be provided. Customer must be provided an option to opt-in or opt-out.
